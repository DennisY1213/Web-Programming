Company Profile

Domex was founded in 2009. Since its establishment, it has been committed to providing high-quality turbo-compressor related products and services for professional users in various industrial fields at home and abroad; it is committed to integrating industry technology and product resources to provide customers with A comprehensive solution for air separation compressors.

In 2019, based on the development needs of the industry and the company, Domex established Hangzhou Domex Gas Equipment Engineering Co., Ltd., and set up Domex headquarters in Hangzhou, the central city of industrial gases in China. And in 2021, a compressor skid installation and maintenance factory will be set up in Deqing, Zhejiang.

Hangzhou Domex currently has 65 employees, and its core members are senior practitioners from well-known domestic and foreign turbo compressor companies and air branch companies. Its functional departments have covered marketing, technical solutions, engineering design, project management, Material procurement, quality control, on-site service, spare parts supply, factory skid installation and maintenance services, etc., is an engineering service company with professional technology, complete functions and rich experience.

Since 2014, Domex has cooperated strongly with the Siemens compressor business section and has become the only authorized system integrator of the Siemens compressor business in China. So far, it has provided more than 100 sets of Siemens compressor product sets and on-site services for domestic and foreign customers. ;The business field ranges from 6000Nm3/h ASU to 100000Nm3/h ASU, from instrument power gas station to chemical process equipment; customers are located in various domestic provinces, Southeast Asia, Russia, Turkey and other overseas regions; complete sets of equipment include full skid-mounted units, motor/ Steam turbine single-drive unit, steam turbine one-to-two unit, steam-electric double-drive unit, unit relocation and transformation, etc.

With rich experience in technical design, project management and engineering services, Domex will provide customers with more and more value-added services such as spare parts reserve, maintenance, and energy-saving renovation for the whole life cycle of compressors. Smart industrial tools and management concepts, based on the traditional business cornerstone of equipment complete sets and product operation and maintenance, help Domex customers to achieve stable operation, energy conservation and carbon reduction.
